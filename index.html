Food and Culture - The Story of Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, or Murgh Makhani, is more than just a dish; it’s a culinary story that reflects the rich tapestry of Indian culture. Originating in Delhi in the 1950s, Butter Chicken was born out of necessity. Kundan Lal Gujral, the owner of Moti Mahal restaurant, invented this iconic dish to use up leftover tandoori chicken, combining it with a creamy tomato-based sauce.
